About Me

My name is Joey Griffin, a junior at Michigan State University. I am currently studying Media and Information, looking to major in Media and Information Science and Management. I live in East Lansing, Michigan, and enjoy the city life and and working. I grew up in the Bay Area in California, and moved to Michigan during the summer of 2018. After finishing school in my city of Midland, I began college at MSU in East Lansing. I mainly enjoy coding, working on computers, reading, playing games, hiking, and partying with friends. My favorite part of college is the fact that I can meet so many new people and connect with tons of others, and learn how I can establish networks with friends. Another one of my favorite hobbies is watching tv shows and movies. Over the past few years, I have seen many different films and shows. Below is a list of my favorite tv shows and movies:

  • True Detective (2013-2019)
